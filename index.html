About

Drummer & Music Advocate

I am a professional drummer based in Gurgaon, India. I started learning when I was 8; having learnt from teachers from different parts of the world, I have developed a wide skillset in terms of genres and styles. Searching for my style, I stumbled upon jazz fusion - it's been fun exploring this vast genre.

  • Age: 17
  • Degree: Rockschool Grade-8 Distinction

I like listening to music of the likes of Gogo Penguin, Avishai Cohen, and Tigran Hamasyan; my main influences in drumming are Dave Weckl and Larnell Lewis.

I believe that technology should be a supplement to and not a replacement of musicians. Good music is synonymous with good instrumentalists. I also feel that there are many things that we can learn from our past, in terms of how music was produced and how the music industry functioned.
